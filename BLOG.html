2021 Guanyin Eagle - Guanyin line foot
This activity is expected to combine tourism resources such as hiking trails, crossing of birds of prey, green bamboo shoots season, Wendan pomelo season and other tourism resources to handle a series of activities such as Guanyin Mountain climbing fun, Guanyin Guanying, Guanyin Mountain experience sightseeing, etc., with "Lehuo Guanyin Mountain" as the core of the activity. In conjunction with the opening of the Guanyin Mountain Raptor Museum, promote eagle watching and ecological activities; in conjunction with the 2020 Backbone Mountain Tourism Year, promote the Guanyin Mountain hiking trail, apply for Guanyin Mountain trail running, trail top certification, tough guy ridge adult ceremony and other activities; cooperate with seasonal agricultural products and promote Guanyin Mountain green bamboo shoots, Wendan pomelo and other local specialties, and in-depth experience activities of picking bamboo shoots and pomelo will be organized to construct the 2020 Guanyin Guanying-Guanyin walking.
Time: 2021-09-11 ~ 2021-10-31
Organizer: Tourism Bureau, Ministry of Transport
